advantages
The operational advantages of bags made of polymer materials are their waterproof , which is especially important for storing products in open areas, as well as in resistance to various acids and other chemicals. Polymer bags have a higher impact strength and strength. Having light weight, but greater strength, polyethylene bags can withstand heavy loads. In a package weighing only 6-7 grams, you can easily put 6.5 kg of cargo - about 1000 times the weight of the package.
application area
Polymer bags have found wide application in all countries of the world for packing, transporting and storing a wide range of bulk products: chemical products, synthetic detergents, polymer resins, mineral fertilizers, cement, as well as some food products: flour, cereals, sugar and starch. Bags made of antistatic films are suitable for the packaging of dangerous goods.
